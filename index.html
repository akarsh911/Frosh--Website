About Us

Frosh is a vibrant and enthusiastic group of individuals dedicated to welcoming the first-year students of TIET with open arms. The Society is committed to ensuring that the transition from high school to university is smooth and enjoyable for all students, and works tirelessly to provide them with the necessary support and resources to achieve success.
At Frosh Week Society, we understand that the first year of university can be overwhelming and daunting, which is why we are committed to making the experience as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. Our team of experienced volunteers is always on hand to provide guidance and support, whether it be through in-person counselling, organising orientation programs, or answering any questions students may have through our infodesk.
Our most exciting event of the year is undoubtedly the Frosh Week, a week-long celebration of all things first-year! During Frosh Week, students get the opportunity to meet their peers, explore the campus, and take part in a range of exciting activities and events designed to promote social integration and foster a sense of community. From fun icebreaker games to informative campus tours, Frosh Week has something for everyone.
We are passionate about making a positive impact on the lives of first-year students, and we believe that our efforts help to create a welcoming and inclusive community that fosters academic and personal growth. By joining Frosh Week Society, students not only get to experience the excitement and camaraderie of Frosh Week, but also have the chance to make meaningful connections and contribute to the success of their fellow classmates.
